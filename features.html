All-in-One Property Management Software

At Propertygaga, we offer a suite of features designed to simplify every aspect of property management:

Circular and Announcement

Keep your tenants and staff informed with our Circular and Announcement feature. Easily create and distribute important notices, updates, and announcements to all relevant parties. Whether it's a maintenance schedule, policy change, or community event, our platform ensures timely and effective communication. Choose between email, SMS, or in-app notifications to reach your audience.

Task Management

Efficiently manage all your property-related tasks with our robust Task Management feature. Assign tasks to team members, set deadlines, and track progress in real-time. Whether it’s routine maintenance, tenant requests, or administrative duties, our platform ensures that nothing slips through the cracks. Prioritize tasks, receive notifications for upcoming deadlines, and stay organized with a clear overview of all ongoing activities.

Accounting Management

Take control of your property’s finances with our integrated Accounting Management system. Record transactions, manage expenses, and reconcile accounts, all within a single platform. Generate financial statements, track income and expenditures, and ensure compliance with accounting standards. Our tools are designed to simplify your financial management, giving you a clear view of your property’s financial health.

Invoice Management

Streamline your invoicing process with our Invoice Management feature. Create, send, and track invoices to tenants, vendors, and service providers. Customize invoices with your branding, set due dates, and monitor payment statuses in real-time. Our system helps you reduce administrative workload, minimize errors, and ensure timely payments.

Visitor Management

Enhance security and streamline the check-in process with our Visitor Management feature. Record and monitor all visitors to your property, ensuring that only authorized individuals gain access. Our system allows you to pre-register visitors, track entry and exit times, and maintain a digital log for future reference. Keep your property secure and your tenants safe with this efficient management tool.

Facilities Management

Manage all property facilities with ease using our Facilities Management feature. Schedule and track maintenance activities, monitor usage, and ensure that all amenities are functioning optimally. Whether it's a gym, swimming pool, or communal area, our software helps you manage bookings, resolve issues quickly, and maintain high standards for your property’s facilities.

Vendor Management

Simplify your relationships with external service providers through our Vendor Management tools. Maintain a centralized database of all vendors, track contracts, and monitor performance. Assign work orders, manage payments, and evaluate vendor efficiency all within the platform. Our system ensures that you work with reliable vendors and receive the best services for your property.

Reports

Make informed decisions with detailed reports generated by our software. Access real-time data on financial performance, occupancy rates, maintenance activities, and more. Customize reports to focus on the metrics that matter most to you. With our easy-to-read visualizations and comprehensive data, you can quickly analyze trends and optimize your property management strategy.
